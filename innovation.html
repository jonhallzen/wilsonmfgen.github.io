INNOVATION

Where's There's Wilson, There's A Way.
Our Machine-Sharpened Dies Are Just One Example.

Never say never. From the simplest to more complex and advanced tooling applications, that’s the Wilson way. And with over 30 years’ experience and groundbreaking ingenuity for the flexographic industry, we’re more than ready to take on any rotary tooling task.

  • For greater customer productivity, Wilson pioneered the use of machine-sharpened dies (MSD), and we continue to pave the way with new innovations in MSD technologies.
  • Today, we remain the only company offering machine-sharpened and hardened dies for better performance and longer life.
  • For the benefit of our customers, Wilson’s R&D team continuously develops proprietary machinery and production techniques that are truly unique—and not available from anyone else.
  • Developing cutting-edge technologies and process advancements assures you consistently receive premium quality with every part.
  • Over the years, we’ve been awarded numerous patents for technology improvements within the industry.
  • For greater convenience and customer support, Wilson leads the field with the industry’s most advanced real-time services, featuring online quoting, ordering, production status, daily email notifications, plus shipment tracking.

At Wilson, we recognize that true innovation and experience, backed by solid service, is the best way to build long-term partnerships with our customers.

So whether it’s a simple cylinder, rotary die, or perhaps a part never seen before, count on Wilson for an ingenious solution. In fact, our Design Task Force* is exclusively dedicated to the most challenging and difficult customer requests.


* Our Design Task Force (DTF) is composed of a select, highly experienced team of Wilson engineers, IT staff, technicians, customer service specialists and sales representatives.
