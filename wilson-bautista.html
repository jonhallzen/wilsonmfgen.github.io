WILSON BAUTISTA

After moving to Canada in 1998 Wilson’s first job was with a screw and fasteners manufacturer. In 2000 he found himself in the die cutting industry and fell in love with it, because of the new challenges he faced every day and solving them. Eventually he became the process manager with duties to find ways to improve our processes and increase productivity but still maintaining the quality of our product.

Wilson joined Wilson Manufacturing in 2013 and has undergone training with our processes and procedures for him to see and understand what it takes to be the Plant manager of our Canadian facility. He is motivated servicing our customer’s needs while maintaining balance with the Company’s interest.

With my spare time I love going downhill mountain biking. In my past life I owed and operated a professional sounds and lighting system and a DJ.
