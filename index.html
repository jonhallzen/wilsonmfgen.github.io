WELCOME TO WILSON!

The Wilson story begins back in 1979, when my father John and I first founded the company to custom build punching equipment.

Initially, our family-owned company served only the St. Louis region with a limited number of products. However, since that time Wilson Manufacturing has evolved into a full-service supplier for the flexographic industry, custom-manufacturing rotary dies, flexible dies, printing cylinders, removable sheeters and many more products. We’ve also grown to service international customers as well.

But while we have the capabilities and strength of many larger facilities, we still operate a family-owned business, offering you the personal service and responsiveness of a smaller company. Today, we take great pride in our superior parts quality and industry-leading innovation—all backed by prompt, professional service our customers have come to expect from us.

In fact, over the years, John and I have earned several patents in our pursuit for improved technologies and quality. Thanks to all the new technologies developed by our team of highly skilled R&D people, we continue that pursuit today. Just as our industry challenges us, I challenge everyone here at Wilson and together we continue to deliver the highest-quality tooling available.

The success of our customers is truly our main mission. And that’s why we’ve remained on the cutting edge in the flexographic industry for over 30 years!
