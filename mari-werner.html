MARI WERNER

Mari is fully committed to building long-term partnerships with her customers—working not only to understand their needs, but to deliver the best solution and service possible. Since joining the Wilson team in 2000, she has become especially proficient assisting customers in the key areas of specialty tooling and processes.

For equally outstanding service and attention to detail, Mari’s customers also depend on Tonia Boswell at Wilson. Mari's territory includes Minnesota, Wisconsin, Iowa, Nebraska, Colorado, Utah, Arizona, North Dakota, and South Dakota. For fun and recreation, Mari dedicates most of her free time to her family and pet dog.

"Bring me any challenge and I will find a way to make it work."
